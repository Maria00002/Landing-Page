Travelling in Switzerland
Switzerland is a popular travel destination known for its stunning Alpine scenery and efficient public transportation.
Top Scenic Train Troutes in Switzerland
The Glaciver Express is a direct Panorama Express train connecting two major mountain resorts of Zermatt and St.Moritz via Andermatt in the central Swiss Alps.
The Bernina Express connects Chur to Tirano in Italy by crossing the Swiss Engadin Alps. The train runs along the Rhaetian Railway in the Albula/Bernina Landscapes.
The GoldenPass Train routes in the Swiss Alps with its base in Montreux. It starts in picturesque Interlaken and ends in Montreux.
The Gotthard Panorama Express connects Lucerne to Lugano, crossing the Swiss Alps from North to South through the Gotthard charset tunnel.
